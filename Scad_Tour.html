IxDA | Interaction 08/ Program/ Darja Isaksson

SCAD Tour

A lunchtime tour of several SCAD buildings will include the Gulfstream Center for Furniture and Industrial Design, a former warehouse with industry-standard studio and production facilities; Hamilton Hall, which houses the film and television and sound design departments; and Montgomery Hall, a former carriage factory repurposed as high-tech studios and classrooms for animation, visual effects, interactive design and game development, and broadcast design and motion graphics departments.

You need to sign up for the tour in advance by logging into RegOnline using the link you received with your confirmation and adding the tour to your registration.

Please contact interaction08(at)ixda.org if you have any questions or need any assistance.
