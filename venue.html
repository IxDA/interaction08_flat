About Savannah

Founded in 1733, Savannah is filled with Southern charm and hospitality. Hip, historic, robust and refined, Savannah is where you’ll find amazing architecture, spooky cemeteries and a rich history. You’ll also discover a hard-partying city with global sophistication, funky nightlife and fabulous food.

Getting to Savannah

Direct flights are available on Delta airlines from many cities in the US, including Boston, New York, Chicago, Washington DC, and Dallas. Flights from other cities in Europe, Asia, and the US usually connect in Atlanta for the short hop on to Savannah.

About SCAD

The Savannah College of Art and Design was founded in 1978 with a curriculum designed to provide an excellent arts education and effective career preparation for students.

SCAD's unique urban campus includes classrooms, studios, computer labs, darkrooms, galleries, libraries, theaters, residence halls, dining facilities and fitness centers. Adaptive reuse of many remarkable structures has helped preserve an important part of Savannah while providing the college with facilities that serve as a living laboratory for the study of the arts, architecture and design.
