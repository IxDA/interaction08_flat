Historic Tour of Savannah

Savannah architectural history tours will be led by SCAD faculty members Robin Williams, Ph.D.; E.G. Daves Rossell, Ph.D.; David Gobel, Ph.D.; Karl Schuler, Ph.D.; and Jeff Eley.

Tour routes include:
The Savannah waterfront, City Hall, Johnson Square and City Market
"Monumental Savannah" and the Bull Street corridor

The tour begins at 4:15p on Friday before the opening reception.
You will be picked up for the at the upstairs of the River Club where the registration desk will be.

You need to sign up for the tour in advance by logging into RegOnline using the link you received with your confirmation and adding the tour to your registration.

Please contact interaction08(at)ixda.org if you have any questions or need any assistance.
