Thank you, sponsors!

The sponsors of the Interaction08 | Savannah conference design some of the world’s most ubiquitous and influential products, services and campaigns. They have shown their commitment and support to interaction design and to IxDA and we’d like to let you know who they are.

Our Platinum sponsors:

Microsoft designs the world’s most ubiquitous operating system, as well as a host of business and consumer tools, and mobile and entertainment environments. They are our host for the Saturday evening reception. It is going to be quite the fest with gaming and Karaoke (XBox Style).

SAP provides enterprise software applications and collaborative business solutions for organizations of all sizes. Their hospitality at the opening reception on Friday night at 7pm will include a Jazz Band and fine Southern food and drinks.

Cooper is a renowned design consultancy that also offers courses and certification in its methods. Cooper is sponsoring the Saturday morning breakfast.

Crispin Porter + Bogusky is a full-service advertising agency with more than 650 employees. CP+B’s interactive department continues to push boundaries with interactive development for leading brands, including Volkswagen, Nike, Sprite, Coke Zero, Burger King, and Domino’s. Past projects of note include simpsonizeme.com, subservientchicken.com, comeclean.com, MINI’s roofstudio.com, sublymonal.com, and vw.com. CP+B is sponsoring the Sunday morning breakfast, and will give a short presentation there.

Autodesk provides industrial design and 3D modeling tools that enable their customers to see and experience their designs before executing them. They are the multi-media sponsor for the conference.

Our Gold Sponsor is Intuit, where the user experience design community is at the heart of the company’s work on new solutions.

Our Silver sponsors are:

  • Avenue A | Razorfish
  • Axure
  • Coroflot 
  • frog design
  • Mad*Pow
  • Motorola Enterprise Mobility: Innovation & Design
  • TandemSeven

Our Bronze sponsors are Apogee, Blackbaud, Blink Interactive, FatDUX, Rosenfeld Media, and Writers Workshop/UA.

Our Media partners are Boxes and Arrows and Core77.

Thank you to all our sponsors, to our partners and to our host, the Savannah College of Art & Design.

 Revised 1/12/08: Added Autodesk, Blackbaud, and Writers Workshop/UA.
