]]> http://interaction08.ixda.org/blog/?feed=rss2&p=65 0 Workshops still open http://interaction08.ixda.org/blog/?p=62 http://interaction08.ixda.org/blog/?p=62#comments Mon, 21 Jan 2008 07:01:31 +0000 http://interaction08.ixda.org/blog/?p=62 If you have been considering signing up for a workshop or two on Friday, February 8th, you still can, provided that you are already registered for the conference.

The morning workshops are:

  • From Data to Difference with Marc Rettig and Jenna Date
  • Effect Maps for Interaction Designers with Darja Isaksson

The afternoon workshops are:

  • Agile and Interaction Design with Jeff Patton and Josh Evnin
  • Paper Prototyping with Todd Warfel

You can get to your current registration record from your confirmation e-mail message.

Once there, if you click on Update Registration, confirm your e-mail address and enter your password, you’ll be offered a link to Make Changes. Under Edit Agenda & Fees, you can change which workshops you are signed up for.

  • You can still participate before the conference by visiting the Connect area of the conference site (http://interaction08.crowdvine.com/), where attendees and observers can meet and exchange messages with one another.
  • After the conference, we are planning to post recordings of many of the presentations.

Thank you everyone who is attending and supporting this event. It is going to be amazing!!!!
