Public Transportation

Chatham Area Transit (CAT)
CAT buses run to and from the airport on a daily basis, servicing many locations in the Savannah Area. Please visit CAT’s web site or call (912) 233-5767 for details and schedules. The CAT Teleride transportation system is a door-to-door transportation service available to eligible disabled persons in Chatham County. For details and information on eligibility, call (912) 354-6900. Yellow Cab has a handicap accessible taxi available. Call 236-1133 and request cab #14. Advance notification requested.
