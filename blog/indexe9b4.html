Wait-listed? Connect instead!

We’ve reached our limit for registered attendees at Interaction08, and the waitlist is growing.If you’re on the waitlist, please consider establishing a presence on the Connect area of the conference site. After the conference, we’re planning to post recordings of the talks. Even if you are not able to attend, you will be able to replay the talks and discuss them.

We’d love to expand the conference, but we’re only able to take 400 people this year. That means that if you’re on the waitlist, you should assume that we won’t be able to open up a slot for you. But we mean it when we invite you to stay connected.
