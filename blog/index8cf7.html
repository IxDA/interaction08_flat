Our primary hotel is sold out – New Hotels!

IxDA has procured 25 rooms at the Doubletree and 25 rooms at the Hilton for the rate of $145.00/night. Both of these hotels are located at the corner of Bay Street and Martin Luther King Jr. Blvd. They sit
adjacent to the River Club, where the conference will be held.

We recommend calling the hotels directly and when calling either hotel, just let them know you are with the IxDA Conference.
